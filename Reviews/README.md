# Reviews

This directory contains short scholarly reviews written as part of my internal
research workflow (Pre‑Basque diachrony, historical linguistics, typology).

## Contents

### • *Review_Egurtzegi_Metathesis.pdf*
Two‑page analytical review of Ander Egurtzegi’s forthcoming chapter **“Metathesis”**
(Wiley–Blackwell Companion to Diachronic Linguistics).  
Focus: extensions beyond Blevins & Garrett (1998, 2004), empirical grounding,
regularity and gradualness, and the introduction of reciprocal metathesis.

### • *Review_Egurtzegi_Metathesis.tex*
LaTeX source for the PDF version.

## Purpose

These reviews are **working notes**, not publications.  
They serve as internal documentation for ongoing research and are versioned here
for clarity, traceability, and future reference.

## Citation

If needed, cite the original work:

- Egurtzegi, Ander. *Metathesis*. Forthcoming in the **Wiley–Blackwell Companion to Diachronic Linguistics**.

The review itself is not intended for external citation.
